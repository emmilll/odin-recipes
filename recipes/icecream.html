Cottage Cheese Apple Pie

HOME

Traditional apple pie meets 1930's superfood cottage cheese in this delectable custard pie that is sure to inspire conversation.

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 cup Sugar
  • ½ cup Cream
  • ¾ cup Milk
  • 1 tsp Vanilla
  • 1 cup Cottage Cheese
  • 3 Apples
  • ½ tsp Cinnamon
  • ¼ tsp Nutmeg
  • 1 Pie Crust

Steps

  1. MIX
  2. BAKE
  3. EAT
